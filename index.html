Character Bio

Hello! I’m Ahmad Ilyas, an Electronics and Communication Engineering student at ZHCET, Aligarh Muslim University.

My main field of interest? Robotics. Everything goes for me, from a soft actuator to a quadruped to a full-on XR Robot. When I'm not trying to make a robot do my bidding, you can find me wrangling analog circuits, sketching out IC designs, or getting lost in the world of embedded systems and IoT. Oh, and I have a full-blown addiction to 3D printing. There’s just something magical about turning digital dreams into tangible objects!

My off-duty passions include building (and occasionally crashing) RC planes, baking, cooking, and playing too many video games (Could you tell?). I also happen to be a massive weeb and an even bigger foodie.

Whether I’m concocting a new robot or attempting the perfect chocolate chip cookie (because why not add a sprinkle of sweetness to engineering?), you can bet I’m always cooking up something exciting.
